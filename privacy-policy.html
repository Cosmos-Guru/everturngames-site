Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

EverTurn Games LLC ("EverTurn Games," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website everturngames.com and publishes mobile applications on the App Store and Google Play (collectively, the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using our Services you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

01 Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Name and email address submitted through our contact form.
  • Any message content or other information you voluntarily send us.

Information collected automatically:

  • Website analytics — We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as pages visited, time on site, browser type, and approximate geographic region. This data does not identify you personally.
  • Mobile app advertising — Our mobile games use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect the Android Advertising ID (AAID) or iOS Advertising Identifier (IDFA), IP address, approximate location derived from IP, device model and operating system version, and app interaction data in order to serve, measure, and attribute advertisements. For users located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the app displays a consent prompt (via Google's User Messaging Platform) allowing you to choose between personalized and non-personalized advertising and to withdraw or change consent at any time through the app's settings. See Google's Privacy Policy and the AdMob partner policy for full details.
  • Cookies & similar technologies — Our website uses cookies for analytics purposes. You can instruct your browser to refuse cookies; some site features may not function correctly if you do so.

02 How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries submitted through our contact form.
  • To understand how visitors use our website and improve our content.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties, ever.

06 Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have specific rights regarding personal information we or our advertising partners hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@everturngames.com.

EEA, UK & Switzerland

Under the GDPR and UK GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, and object to the processing of your personal data, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority. Within our mobile apps, you can change your advertising consent at any time through the in-app consent prompt provided by Google's User Messaging Platform.

California Residents

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion or correction of your information, and opt out of the "sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising (i.e., personalized ads). To opt out within our mobile apps, adjust ad personalization through the in-app consent prompt, or disable personalized advertising in your device settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising). You may also email us to exercise these rights. We will not discriminate against you for exercising any CCPA right.

Other U.S. State Privacy Laws

Residents of states with comprehensive privacy laws (including Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Texas, and others) have similar rights to access, delete, correct, and opt out of targeted advertising. Contact us at the address above to exercise these rights.
